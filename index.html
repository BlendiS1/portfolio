About

About Me

I'm a UI/UX Designer from Kosovo. I've worked across product design, frontend development, and no-code, so I know how things get built and I design with that in mind.

Right now I'm at Broadleaf Commerce as RevOps Lead, and I take on freelance design projects on the side. I enjoy working with early-stage founders who have a product idea and need someone to shape how it looks and feels.

I'm drawn to mobile apps and anything where good design actually makes a difference to how people use the product.
